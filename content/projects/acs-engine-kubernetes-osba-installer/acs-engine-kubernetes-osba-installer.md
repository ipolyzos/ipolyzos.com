[acs-engine-kubernetes-osba-installer](https://github.com/null-box/acs-engine-kubernetes-osba-installer) automates the installation of the [Open Service Broker for Azure (OSBA)](https://github.com/Azure/open-service-broker-azure) on [ACS-Engine](https://github.com/Azure/acs-engine)-based Kubernetes clusters. It enables on-demand provisioning and binding of Azure managed services (databases, queues, caches) directly from Kubernetes workloads.
