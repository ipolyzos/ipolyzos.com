[bosh-bootloader-snap](https://github.com/ipolyzos/bosh-bootloader-snap) is a [Snap](https://snapcraft.io/) package for the [BOSH Bootloader (bbl)](https://github.com/cloudfoundry/bosh-bootloader) CLI, enabling one-command installation on any Snapcraft-enabled Linux distribution. [BOSH](https://bosh.io) is the Cloud Foundry foundation for release engineering, deployment, and lifecycle management across large VM fleets.
