[kind-playground](https://github.com/ipolyzos/kind-playground) is a platform for rapid prototyping and idea experimentation built on [KinD (Kubernetes in Docker)](https://kind.sigs.k8s.io/). Its core focus is on user-friendly exploration in a local environment. Components are not intended for production use.
